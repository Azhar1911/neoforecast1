Bitcoin Wave Structure Update

Latest NeoWave count suggests Bitcoin is completing a complex correction with multiple timeframe analysis revealing flat pattern formation and potential triangle development.

I'm using two main charts:

  1. The 6-month chart, which shows the long-term trend since the beginning.
  2. The monthly chart, which focuses on price movements over the past few years.

Looking at the 6-month chart, I think Bitcoin is following a "flat" pattern, which has three waves: A, B, and C. Right now, we're in wave B. I expect wave B to end around $156,000. If Bitcoin goes above that say, to $160,000 or $170,000 then this pattern probably isn't a flat, and we'd have to consider other possibilities.

On the monthly chart, you can see the internal structure of waves A and B of the flat that you observed on the 6 monthly chart. Focusing on the recent uptrend that started in August 2024 from $49,000, from the point marked X towards the right end, we saw a strong move up to $108,000, then a drop to $74,000. I'm calling these moves A and B, and the current rally to new highs which is ongoing this very minute is labelled wave C.

To figure out what pattern this is, I checked the weekly chart for internal details about the A and B that was followed after the X wave. It strongly looks like waves A and B are the first two legs of a triangle. If this is right, after the current rally ends, we should see a drop (wave D), followed by one last push upward (wave E).
