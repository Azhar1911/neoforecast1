S&P 500 Major Reversal Pattern Confirmed

December 15, 2024
5 min read
Neo Forecasts

Executive Summary

Our comprehensive NeoWave analysis of the S&P 500 has identified a critical reversal pattern that signals a significant shift in market dynamics. Following the Trump tariff-induced decline, the index has formed a complex corrective structure that suggests the completion of a major wave cycle.

Key Finding

The S&P 500 has completed a complex flat correction pattern, with wave C targeting the 4,200-4,300 support zone. This represents a high-probability reversal area where institutional buying is expected to emerge.

Technical Analysis

Wave Structure Analysis

The current decline from the November highs exhibits the characteristics of a three-wave corrective pattern (A-B-C), rather than an impulsive five-wave structure. This distinction is crucial as it suggests the overall uptrend remains intact.

Wave A completed at the 4,600 level, followed by a complex wave B retracement that reached 4,850. The current wave C is approaching its projected completion zone, creating an attractive risk-reward setup for long positions.

NeoWave Confirmation Signals

Several NeoWave-specific indicators support this analysis:

  • Time relationships between waves A and C are approaching equality
  • Volume patterns show climactic selling characteristics
  • Momentum divergence at key support levels
  • Fibonacci retracement confluence at the 61.8% level

Critical Price Levels

  • Primary Support Zone 4,200 - 4,300
  • Secondary Support 4,150
  • Resistance Level 1 4,650
  • Resistance Level 2 4,850
  • Target Zone 5,200 - 5,400

Market Outlook

The completion of this corrective phase is expected to mark the beginning of the next impulsive wave higher. Based on NeoWave projections, the subsequent rally could target the 5,200-5,400 zone over the next 3-6 months.

This analysis aligns with broader market fundamentals, including improving economic indicators and potential policy shifts that could support equity valuations in 2025.

Risk Management

A decisive break below 4,150 would invalidate this bullish scenario and suggest a deeper correction is underway. Traders should implement appropriate stop-loss levels and position sizing based on their risk tolerance.

Conclusion

The S&P 500 is approaching a critical juncture where the completion of a major corrective pattern coincides with strong technical support. This creates an asymmetric risk-reward opportunity for investors with a medium-term horizon.

As always, market conditions can change rapidly, and this analysis should be combined with proper risk management and position sizing strategies. Stay tuned for updates as this pattern develops.
