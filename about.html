My Journey to NeoWave Mastery

From skeptical beginner to precision forecaster - the evolution of a market analyst

The Path to Precision

2019

The Beginning

Started my journey in financial markets with basic technical analysis - trendlines, support and resistance levels. Began following US stock markets and crypto with intense curiosity.

2020-2021

The Skeptical Phase

My father suggested Elliott Wave theory, but I dismissed it as "dumb and too much work." Little did I know this would become my passion.

2022

The Awakening

A free YouTube video promised I could forecast future price action. Excited, I learned everything about Elliott Wave in 24 hours and applied it religiously for 6-7 months.

2023

The Disappointment

Despite following Elliott Wave experts and applying the theory diligently, the accuracy I desired remained elusive. The results weren't meeting my expectations.

2024

The Discovery

I discovered Glen Neely's NeoWave. Initially overwhelming, but once I witnessed its application and accuracy, I fell in love. I practiced day and night, even taking live classes with Glen Neely himself.

2025

The Mastery

Today, my forecasts are increasingly accurate. I've expanded into forex markets and now aim to build a community of like-minded individuals to share knowledge and grow together.

The NeoWave Difference

What sets NeoWave apart from traditional Elliott Wave analysis is its precision and systematic approach. While Elliott Wave gave me a foundation, NeoWave provided the accuracy I was desperately seeking.

"I even took live classes with Glen Neely to deepen my understanding of his methodologies. This direct learning experience transformed my analytical capabilities."

The journey from basic technical analysis to NeoWave mastery wasn't easy, but every challenge taught me valuable lessons about market behavior, patience, and the importance of continuous learning.
